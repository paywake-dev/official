Description of the Program

The Program allows users to create an incentive for waking up by agreeing to forfeit a predetermined amount of US dollars that they choose (the “Amount”) if they fail to take certain actions to verify they are awake at a certain predetermined date and time (such time, the “Wakeup Time”).  If a user verifies they are awake at the Wakeup Time, they do not forfeit the Amount.  

In addition, users who verify they are awake at the Wakeup Time are eligible to receive a portion (the “Incentive Portion”) of the amounts forfeited by other users (if any) who did not verify they were awake at the Wakeup Time.  The Calculation of the Incentive Portion is described below.

The Program is limited to those dates and times (the “Available Times”) and amounts that we make available via the Company Properties.  Any time at least one user participates in the Program for an Available Time, that instance of the Program is referred to as a “Wakeup.”  Any time you participate in the Program or a Wakeup, the rules in these terms and conditions apply.

Enrollment and Entry

To participate in the Program and a Wakeup, you must select an Available Time from the Paywake.net dashboard in your account.  Once you select an Available Time, you will be prompted to enter an amount that you agree to forfeit if you do not verify you are awake at the Wakeup Time. You must agree to an Amount of at least $5 to enter the Wakeup.  We may impose a maximum amount that you are able to forfeit for any Wakeup at our sole discretion.

You may only enroll in one Wakeup in any 19-hour period.  You must enroll in a Wakeup at least three hours and 1 minute before the applicable Available Time.  For example, if you would like to enroll in a Wakeup for 7:00AM on Monday, January 31, you must enroll in the Wakeup by 3:59AM on Monday, January 1.  Continuing with this example, once you enroll in a Wakeup for 7:00AM on Monday, January 31, you may not participate in another Wakeup until 2:00AM on Tuesday, February 1.

You may only enroll in two Wakeups in a 24-hour period, regardless of when the Wakeups will take place.

Once you enroll in a Wakeup by choosing a Wakeup Time and Amount and we confirm your enrollment, you may not remove yourself from the Wakeup and the Amount will be available to other users in the calculation of the Incentive Portion following the Wakeup.

You will only be enrolled into a Wakeup if you receive a confirmation message via the Company Properties and a confirmation email to the email address we have on file in your Account.  

We may cancel a Wakeup for any reason with notice to its participants.  If for any reason we must cancel a Wakeup, no participant will be required to forfeit their Amount for that Wakeup subject to the terms and conditions contained in this Agreement regarding Fraud and Abuse.

In order to verify you are awake for any Wakeup and to be eligible to receive your Amount and any Incentive Amount, you will be asked to take certain actions that will be described in advance of entering the Wakeup. For example, you may be asked to take photos using your camera phone of up to 10 items commonly found in a home, such as a door handle, tooth brush, trash can, or clothing hanger and transmit them to Paywake for verification.  You may be asked to take additional steps to verify you took these actions and did not enlist some other person or process to subvert the intent of the Wakeup.  Only once Paywake verifies that you took photos of those items will you be eligible to receive the Amount and any Incentive Portion.  You are responsible for ensuring you have at least the items listed above and those listed in advance of entering the Wakeup to verify that you were awake for the Wakeup.

Calculation of Incentive Portion

Except for a 15% service fee payable to Paywake, all amounts forfeited by users in a given Wakeup (the “Total Incentive Package”) will be divided among the users who verified they were awake at the Wakeup Time (each a “Successful Waker”).  The Total Incentive Package will be divided in proportion to the amount each Successful Waker agreed to forfeit out of all of the amounts each Successful Waker agreed to forfeit.  For example, if there are two Successful Wakers for a certain Wakeup, and one agreed to forfeit $40 and the other agreed to forfeit $60, the Successful Waker who agreed to forfeit $40 would receive 40% of the Total Incentive Package because they agreed to forfeit an amount that is 40% of the Total Incentive Package.

Because the amount of the Incentive Portion, if any, depends on the sum of Amounts forfeited for a given Wakeup and the number of Successful Wakers, we will determine the Successful Wakers no later than 72 hours after the applicable Wakeup, and we will determine the amount of any Incentive Portion no later than 7 days following any such Wakeup.
